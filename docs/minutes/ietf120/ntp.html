The NTP working group meeting at IETF 120 covered the status of several documents including the enterprise profile, registries document, NTS for PTP, NTP over PTP, NTPV5 requirements, NTPV5 specifics, and Rough Time. Discussions included addressing IESG comments, coordinating with the 1588 community, and planning hackathon projects. The group emphasized the need for more reviews on the documents.
